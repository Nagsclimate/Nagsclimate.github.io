### Overview

My research focuses on understanding the complex interactions between weather patterns, climate change, and urban environments. I aim to develop practical, data-driven solutions to help cities adapt to the challenges posed by climate extremes, flooding, and sustainable development.

### Key Research Areas

#### 1) **Urban Climate Forecasting:**
My work involves developing weather and climate models to forecast urban climate dynamics, with a particular focus on predicting extreme weather events in rapidly growing cities. I integrate atmospheric models with urban infrastructure data to create more accurate, location-specific forecasts that can guide urban planning and disaster management.

#### 2) **Urban Flood Prediction:**
Using atmospheric models like WRF (Weather Research and Forecasting), I have worked on integrating real-time rainfall data with hydrological models to predict urban flooding events. This work helps to identify vulnerable areas and supports the development of early warning systems for disaster risk reduction.

#### 3) **Climate Risk Assessment:**
I conduct risk assessments for cities, evaluating the potential impacts of climate change on infrastructure, ecosystems, and human populations. My assessments help to identify vulnerable regions and inform climate adaptation strategies that prioritize sustainability and resilience.

#### 4) **Nature-Based Solutions (NbS) for Urban Sustainability:**
In rapidly growing urban environments, I focus on developing nature-based solutions (NbS) that address the challenges of flooding, heat stress, and ecosystem degradation. My research explores how urban green spaces, wetlands, and other natural systems can be integrated into urban planning to reduce climate risks.

#### 5) **Urban Climate Policy Development:**
I am also passionate about shaping climate policies that align with both local and global sustainability goals. My work includes researching best practices in urban climate governance and advocating for policies that promote green infrastructure, reduce carbon emissions, and foster sustainable development.

### Ongoing Projects

- **Urban Resilience through Climate-Data Integration:**  
I’m currently working on a project that combines real-time weather data with socio-economic data to assess urban resilience to climate risks, helping cities develop actionable plans for disaster mitigation and climate adaptation.

- **Modeling Nature-Based Solutions for Flood Management:**  
I am involved in research that explores the role of urban green infrastructure in flood management, assessing the effectiveness of NbS in mitigating the impact of extreme rainfall events in metropolitan areas.

### Future Directions

I am excited about expanding my research into urban climate policies, focusing on how cities can not only adapt to climate change but also play a key role in mitigating its effects. By combining my research with real-world applications, I aim to contribute to the global movement toward sustainable, climate-resilient urbanization.
