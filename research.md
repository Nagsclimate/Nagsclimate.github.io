### Overview

My research focuses on understanding the complex interactions between weather patterns, climate change, and urban environments. I aim to develop practical, data-driven solutions to help cities adapt to the challenges posed by climate extremes, flooding, and sustainable development.

### Key Research Areas

1) ***Urban Climate Forecasting:*** My work involves developing weather and climate models to forecast urban climate dynamics, with a particular focus on predicting extreme weather events in rapidly growing cities. I integrate atmospheric models with urban infrastructure data to create more accurate, location-specific forecasts that can guide urban planning and disaster management.

2) ***Urban Flood Prediction:*** Using atmospheric models like WRF (Weather Research and Forecasting), I have worked on integrating real-time rainfall data with hydrological models to predict urban flooding events. This work helps to identify vulnerable areas and supports the development of early warning systems for disaster risk reduction.
