Hi, I’m **Nagaraju Gaddam**. I’m an Urban Climate Scientist, researcher, and entrepreneur passionate about weather, climate, urban sustainability, and climate policies. Over the years, I’ve worked on a variety of projects focusing on the integration of atmospheric modeling and urban flood forecasting. My goal is to develop practical solutions that help cities adapt to the challenges posed by climate change.

### My Journey

I started my academic path with a deep interest in atmospheric sciences, later pursuing a Master’s in Ocean and Atmospheric Sciences. During my studies, I focused on understanding the impacts of land-use changes and sea surface temperature variations on climate patterns in India. My work has allowed me to contribute to projects on urban climate forecasting, flood prediction, and risk assessments, Nature Based Solutions developent in rapidly growing urban environments.

Currently, I’m exploring ways to combine my research with real-world applications, helping cities become more resilient and sustainable. I’m also driven by a desire to help shape urban climate policies that align with both local and global sustainability goals.When I’m not immersed in my research, I enjoy experimenting with new recipes, reading about innovative solutions in the climate space, and occasionally writing about my experiences.
